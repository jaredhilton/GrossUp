# Privacy Policy for GrossUp

**Last updated: January 23, 2026**

## Overview

GrossUp is a simple calculator app that helps you compute gross and net income amounts after taxes and fees.

## Data Collection

**GrossUp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- No account or sign-up required
- No analytics or tracking
- No network requests
- All calculations are performed locally on your device
- No data is stored between sessions

## Third-Party Services

GrossUp does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

## Children's Privacy

GrossUp does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted to this page with an updated revision date.
